Welcome to Cabello we are a modern, stylish hair & beauty salon located in Halesworth. We are very passionate about what we do & assure you will not be disappointed with the quality of service given.

Our aim is to make our clients feel relaxed & comfortable throughout thier treatments whilst delivering a high quality of service. We believe it is crucial to connect with the client to gain an understanding of thier needs & lifestyle to deliver a style which will be achievable at home as well as in the salon. We deliver the best style & colour to compliment the clients facial shape and features, making the most of there natural beauty.

We pride ourselves in using top leading brands such as, Wella professional colours & styling products, St Tropez, CND shellac and Dermalogica, to provide amazing results each & every time.

We have a highly qualified team & run regular training programmes to keep up with the latest techniques and trends.
